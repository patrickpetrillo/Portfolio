SOCIAL MEDIA VIDEO CONTENT PRODUCTION

The project was developed with an approach focused on producing video content for the promotion of recipes across social media platforms. The objective was to create a clear and effective visual communication, capable of enhancing the product through dynamic and easily consumable imagery, without the use of voiceover. The development focused on the full production process, from video creation to final editing, ensuring stylistic and narrative consistency across all outputs. The decision to remove spoken content led to a rhythm built entirely through editing, music and visual sequencing, making the content direct and universally understandable. The videos were specifically designed for platforms such as Instagram and TikTok, using a vertical format and a structure optimized for short-form content, capable of capturing attention within the first few seconds and maintaining a high level of engagement. Particular attention was given to composition, lighting and detail enhancement, in order to make each recipe visually clear and engaging. The final result is a series of cohesive, high-impact video contents, designed for fast consumption and to effectively support communication across social media platforms.
