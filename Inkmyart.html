Logotype and Visual Identity Design

LOGO RECONSTRUCTION AND VISUAL IDENTITY APPLICATION

The project began with the complete reconstruction of the logotype, developed from a hand-drawn concept provided by the client. The logo was redesigned from scratch in vector format, refining its proportions, improving readability, and enhancing overall visual clarity to ensure a professional, scalable, and versatile result suitable for any application. Following the logo development, a cohesive and structured visual identity was created to strengthen the brand’s distinctive character. The graphic system was built through a consistent color palette, recurring visual elements, and a clear, functional typographic approach. The identity was then applied across a range of communication materials, both printed and promotional, including business cards, flyers, posters, planners, and branded items. Each asset was designed with attention to detail and visual consistency, ensuring a unified and recognizable brand presence. The entire project was developed with the aim of creating a solid and adaptable visual system, capable of enhancing the brand’s presence across both physical environments and everyday communication channels.
