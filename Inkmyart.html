BRAND & VISUAL IDENTITY

The visual identity project was developed with a coherent and targeted approach, aimed at building a strong and recognizable brand image for Ink My Art Tattoo. The entire graphic system originates from the need to communicate the brand’s identity clearly, through a bold and contemporary visual language.

The design is based on an essential color palette — red, black, and white — which helps define a clean yet impactful aesthetic. The use of sharp contrasts and minimal graphic elements enhances readability and ensures strong recognition across all applications.

The visual system has been applied to various materials, including business cards, flyers, posters, branded merchandise, and studio materials, maintaining formal and stylistic consistency throughout. Typography hierarchy and layout structure have been carefully designed to highlight information and improve usability. The result is a solid and versatile visual identity, capable of adapting to different contexts while maintaining a distinctive character, created to strengthen the brand’s presence and communication over time.
