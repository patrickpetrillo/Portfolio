ABOUT ME

I’m a graphic designer working at the intersection of aesthetics and communication, turning ideas into visual content that captures attention, tells stories, and delivers impact. My approach is simple: design that communicates. Every project starts with a clear concept and evolves through visual composition, imagery, and identity, with the goal of creating work that is not only visually strong but also meaningful and memorable. I work with the Adobe Creative Suite to develop branding, advertising, and digital content, from graphic design to visual production for social media and campaigns. I also have experience in print design, managing projects across different formats with a strong focus on quality and detail. My work explores different visual languages, from creative concepts to advertising and more experimental projects. This allows me to adapt to different contexts while maintaining a consistent visual direction. I also integrate UX principles and digital awareness into my process, creating visuals that are not only aesthetically effective but designed to perform in real-world contexts. I’m looking for opportunities where creativity meets strategy, and where design becomes a tool to build identity, communicate ideas, and leave a lasting impression.
