LOGO AND VISUAL IDENTITY DEVELOPMENT

The visual identity project was developed through a structured approach, aimed at defining a solid and recognizable graphic system, capable of clearly representing the reference sector. The objective was to build a visual language able to communicate reliability, precision and technical expertise, while maintaining a contemporary and distinctive aesthetic. The project included the design of the logo from scratch, developed as the core element of the identity. The graphic mark was constructed by integrating an architectural reference within the typographic structure, strengthening the connection with the construction field. The development focused on defining a balanced and highly legible typographic structure, supported by a controlled color palette designed to convey solidity and professionalism. The identity was applied across various touchpoints, including printed materials and practical applications, ensuring visual continuity and recognition across all contexts. The final result is a clear, functional and scalable visual identity, designed to maintain consistency and effectiveness over time.
