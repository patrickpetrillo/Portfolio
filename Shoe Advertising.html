VISUAL CONTENT PRODUCTION

The project was developed with a content-driven approach, aimed at creating visual assets to effectively showcase and promote a custom product. The objective was to build a coherent and recognizable visual language, capable of conveying the product’s craftsmanship through a contemporary and dynamic aesthetic. The development focused on creative direction and the production of photographic and video content, with particular attention to composition, lighting and the construction of a consistent visual atmosphere. The customization process was used as a central narrative element, becoming an integral part of the communication. The video production was specifically designed for social media, using a vertical format and a visual rhythm tailored for short-form content such as reels, optimized for immediate and engaging consumption. Each asset was designed to highlight details, materials and the uniqueness of the product, while maintaining strong visual consistency across different outputs. The final result is a structured and high-impact visual content system, designed to support product promotion across digital and social platforms.
