PHOTO AND VIDEO SHOOT

The video and photography project was developed with a creative and immersive approach, aimed at highlighting the craftsmanship and uniqueness of the hand-painted footwear. The visual narrative alternates between moments of production and presentation scenes, building a dynamic and engaging story.

The use of colored lighting and enveloping atmospheres helps create a contemporary and distinctive aesthetic, enhancing the painted details and the vibrancy of the illustrations. Close-up shots and a focus on the artist’s gestures emphasize the artisanal care and the value of handmade work.

Dynamic editing and visual compositions reinforce the expressive character of the product, transforming the shoe into a narrative element. The result is a visually impactful piece of content, designed to communicate originality and identity across digital channels.
