Rebuild

Logo Design

BRAND IDENTITY FOR CONSTRUCTION SECTOR

The “Rebuild” logo represents a perfect balance between contemporary aesthetics and technical identity. The logotype is designed using a custom typeface, characterized by thin lines and details inspired by architectural drawing, conveying precision, method, and expertise. The integration of a stylized roof profile visually reinforces the company’s core business, which is closely linked to the construction and renovation sector.

The color palette, dominated by dark tones with modern accents, gives the brand a solid, elegant, and highly professional image. Overall, the logo communicates reliability, innovation, and attention to detail, positioning the brand in a distinctive way within the construction industry.
