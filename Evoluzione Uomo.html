VISUAL IDENTITY DEVELOPMENT

The visual identity project was developed through a methodical approach, focused on building a coherent and recognizable graphic system, capable of clearly defining the brand’s positioning. The objective was to create a visual language that is both essential and distinctive, able to convey solidity, attention to detail and formal consistency. The development focused on defining a clean and controlled typographic structure, supported by a minimal, high-contrast color palette. The reference to the Italian tricolor was introduced as an identity element, integrated in a subtle yet meaningful way within the system. The project was applied across multiple touchpoints, including printed materials, apparel and environmental applications, ensuring visual continuity and perceptual consistency. The final result is a solid and flexible visual identity, designed to be consistently applied and to maintain its effectiveness over time.
